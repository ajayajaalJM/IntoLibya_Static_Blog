<h1 class="wp-block-heading">What Is Included in a Libya Tour Package?</h1>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">When planning a trip to Libya, travelers often ask: <strong>“What exactly is included in a tour package?”</strong> At IntoLibya, we offer <strong>all-inclusive and non-inclusive packages</strong>, so you can choose the experience that fits your travel style.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">All-Inclusive Package</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Our <strong>all-inclusive packages</strong> are designed to remove stress and let you focus on exploring Libya. They include:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Visa processing:</strong> Standard visa fees included (free for all-inclusive travelers)</li>



<li><strong>Accommodation:</strong> Hotels (3★ or 4★ where available) and desert tents for Sahara expeditions</li>



<li><strong>Meals:</strong> Breakfast, lunch, dinner, and small snacks (mini food tours included)</li>



<li><strong>Transport:</strong> All transfers within Libya, including airport pick-up and drop-off</li>



<li><strong>Guided tours:</strong> English-speaking guides for all destinations</li>



<li><strong>Entrance fees:</strong> All sites, including Leptis Magna, Sabratha, and Ghadames</li>



<li><strong>Tourist police escorts:</strong> Required by law for all travelers</li>
</ul>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Non-Inclusive Package</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">For those who prefer a lower-cost option, the <strong>non-inclusive package</strong> covers fewer services:</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Not included:</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Visa costs (unless added separately)</li>



<li>Meals</li>



<li>Entrance fees to major ruins (Leptis Magna, Sabratha, Ghadames)</li>



<li>Optional extras (spending money, insurance)</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Non-inclusive packages still provide transport and guided support for safety, but travelers are responsible for additional expenses.</p>
