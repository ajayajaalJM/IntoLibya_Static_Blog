<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Step 2: Collect Sponsor Documents</strong></h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">After paying your deposit, the tour company provides the <strong>official sponsor documents</strong> you need for your eVisa application. These documents confirm your itinerary, hotel accommodations, and local guide arrangements.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Step 3: Apply via the eVisa Portal</strong></h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Once you have your sponsor documents, the next step is to apply for your eVisa through Libya’s official online portal. You will need to upload your documents, provide personal information, and pay the visa fee.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The online system is user-friendly, but having the sponsor documents ready in advance ensures your application is processed quickly and without errors.</p>

<p class="wp-block-paragraph">After submitting your application, you will receive confirmation via email once the eVisa is approved. This confirmation includes a reference number and instructions to present upon arrival in Libya.</p>
