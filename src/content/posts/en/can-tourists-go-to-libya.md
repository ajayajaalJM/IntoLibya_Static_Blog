<h2 class="wp-block-heading">When Is the Best Time to Visit?</h2>

<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The ideal months to travel in Libya are October through April, when temperatures are moderate and desert travel is more comfortable. During this period sightseeing is pleasant, archaeological exploration is feasible, and desert journeys are safer. Conversely, summer months can be extremely hot, especially in southern regions, making travel more challenging and less enjoyable.</p>
