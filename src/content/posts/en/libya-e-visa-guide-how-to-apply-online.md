<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Required Documents</h2>

<p class="wp-block-paragraph">To apply for a Libya e-Visa, you will need:</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Passport with at least 6 months validity</li>

<li>Scanned copy of passport information page</li>

<li>Answer a questionnaire of personal information (occupation / residency)</li>

<li>Proof of sponsorship (provided by IntoLibya)</li>
</ul>
